Everlasting, intricate, and one-of-a-kind—make lasting memories with our brand new Handcrafted Paper Flowers.

Perfect for Mother's Day, birthdays, anniversaries, or every day "just because I love you" moments, these beautiful blooms are handcrafted to impress.

Our Handcrafted Paper Flowers will constantly wow and never wilt as no water is needed to keep these allergy-friendly blooms bright. With every petal made by hand from premium paper, each arrangement is unique—just like real roses, but these will last a lifetime.

What's included:

6 stemmed paper roses

3 stemmed paper baby's breath

3 paper mint leaf stems

2 paper eucalyptus stems

1 white ribbon to complete the arrangement

1 beautiful box for shipping

Our Handcrafted Paper Flowers are 13 inches in length, with the flower petals being 1.7 inches in height.
